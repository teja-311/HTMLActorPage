Vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works predominantly in Tamil cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in India and has a significant fan following. Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Vetri" (1984) and later appeared in several films as a lead actor. Some of his notable films include "Ghilli," "Thuppakki," "Mersal," and "Master." Known for his charismatic screen presence and dance skills, Vijay has won numerous awards for his performances. In addition to his acting career, he is also involved in philanthropic activities and has a strong influence on social issues in Tamil Nadu.
